HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY - COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE

Policy Coverage:
- Annual Maximum: $500,000
- Lifetime Maximum: $2,000,000
- Deductible: $1,000 per year
- Out-of-pocket Maximum: $5,000 per year

Covered Services:
1. Hospital Confinement: $200 per day, maximum 365 days per year
2. Surgical Procedures: Based on fee schedule
   - Minor surgery: $500-$1,000
   - Major surgery: $2,000-$5,000
3. Emergency Room Visits: 80% coverage after deductible
4. Prescription Drugs: 80% coverage with $25 copay
5. Preventive Care: 100% coverage, no deductible

Pre-existing Conditions:
- Waiting period: 12 months
- Conditions must be disclosed at application
- Coverage begins after 12-month waiting period

Exclusions:
1. Cosmetic procedures (unless medically necessary)
2. Experimental treatments
3. Treatments not approved by medical board
4. Self-inflicted injuries
5. Drug or alcohol rehabilitation (unless covered under mental health rider)

Mental Health Coverage:
- Inpatient care: Up to 30 days per year
- Outpatient therapy: Up to 20 sessions per year
- Coverage: 80% after deductible

Maternity Coverage:
- Prenatal care: 100% coverage
- Delivery: 80% coverage after deductible
- Postnatal care: Up to 6 weeks

Claim Submission:
- Submit within 90 days of service
- Include itemized bills and medical records
- Pre-authorization required for hospitalization
- Processing time: 15-30 days
