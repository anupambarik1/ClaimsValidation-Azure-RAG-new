MOTOR INSURANCE POLICY - COMPREHENSIVE AUTO COVERAGE

Policy Coverage:
- Vehicle Value: Actual Cash Value or Agreed Value
- Coverage Period: 12 months renewable
- Geographic Coverage: United States and Canada

Coverage Types:

1. COLLISION COVERAGE
- Damage from accidents with other vehicles or objects
- Deductible: $500
- Maximum coverage: Actual cash value of vehicle
- Includes single-vehicle accidents

2. COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE
- Theft or attempted theft
- Vandalism and malicious mischief
- Weather damage (hail, flood, wind)
- Fire and explosion
- Animal collisions
- Glass breakage
- Deductible: $250

3. LIABILITY COVERAGE
- Bodily Injury: $100,000 per person, $300,000 per accident
- Property Damage: $50,000 per accident
- Covers legal fees and court costs

4. PERSONAL INJURY PROTECTION (PIP)
- Medical expenses: Up to $10,000 per person
- Lost wages: Up to 80% of income, maximum $2,000/month
- Funeral expenses: Up to $5,000

Exclusions:
1. Intentional damage or illegal activities
2. Racing or speed contests
3. Driving under influence of drugs or alcohol
4. Use for commercial delivery without commercial rider
5. Damage during vehicle use by unauthorized driver
6. Normal wear and tear, mechanical breakdown
7. Tire damage (unless from covered accident)

Additional Benefits:
- Towing and labor: Up to $75 per towing, maximum 10 events per year
- Rental reimbursement: $75/day for maximum 10 days following covered loss
- Roadside assistance: Included at no extra cost

Glass Coverage:
- Windshield repair: $100 deductible waiver
- Windshield replacement: Full deductible applies
- Other glass (windows, mirrors): $100 deductible

Claim Process:
1. Report accident to police (if required by law)
2. Notify insurance company within 24 hours
3. Submit photos of damage
4. Obtain repair estimates (minimum 2 estimates)
5. Authorization for repairs within 3 business days
6. Processing time: 5-10 business days after inspection

Discounts Available:
- Multi-vehicle discount: 10%
- Safe driver (no claims 3 years): 15%
- Anti-theft device: 5%
- Bundled with home insurance: 10%
