DEATH BENEFIT:
Base Death Benefit: $250,000
Accumulated Dividends: Added to death benefit
Cash Value: Accumulates over time (not added to death benefit)
Guaranteed Death Benefit: Never decreases

CASH VALUE ACCUMULATION:
- Begins accumulating after first year
- Guaranteed minimum interest: 3% annually
- Current credited rate: 4.5% annually
- Tax-deferred growth
- Available for loans or withdrawals

LOAN PROVISIONS:
- Can borrow up to 90% of cash value
- Interest rate: 5% annually
- No credit check or approval needed
- Loan reduces death benefit if unpaid
- Interest can be paid or added to loan balance

DIVIDEND OPTIONS (Non-Guaranteed):
1. Cash payment
2. Premium reduction
3. Purchase paid-up additional insurance
4. Accumulate at interest
5. Repay policy loans

PAID-UP INSURANCE OPTION:
- After 10 years, can stop paying premiums
- Policy remains in force with reduced death benefit
- Based on accumulated cash value

ACCELERATED DEATH BENEFIT RIDERS:
Terminal Illness Rider:
- Available if life expectancy < 12 months
- Access up to 90% of death benefit
- No additional premium

Chronic Illness Rider:
- Available if unable to perform 2 of 6 ADLs for 90+ days
- Monthly benefit up to 2% of death benefit
- Maximum 50% of death benefit over lifetime

Long-Term Care Rider:
- Qualified long-term care expenses
- Up to $5,000 per month
- Reduces death benefit dollar-for-dollar

EXCLUSIONS:
- Suicide within first 2 years: Premium refund only
- After 2 years: Incontestable

PREMIUM PAYMENT:
- Guaranteed never to increase
- Grace period: 31 days
- If premium not paid: Cash value can maintain policy
- Automatic premium loan option available

WAIVER OF PREMIUM RIDER:
- If totally disabled for 6+ months before age 65
- Company waives premiums during disability
- Policy continues with full benefits
- Must provide proof of disability annually
- Additional premium: $15/month

ACCIDENTAL DEATH BENEFIT RIDER:
- Additional $250,000 if death by accident before age 70
- Accident must occur and death result within 180 days
- Excludes: Aviation (non-commercial), war, suicide, illegal activities
- Additional premium: $25/month

CLAIM REQUIREMENTS - STANDARD DEATH CLAIM:
- Certified death certificate (original)
- Completed claimant's statement
- Policy document (original)
- Proof of beneficiary identity
- Attending physician's statement

CLAIM REQUIREMENTS - ACCELERATED BENEFITS:
Terminal Illness:
- Physician certification of terminal condition
- Life expectancy statement
- Complete medical records
- Attending physician statement

Chronic Illness:
- Assessment by licensed healthcare practitioner
- Plan of care
- Proof of inability to perform ADLs
- Medical records documenting condition

Long-Term Care:
- Certification of care need
- Itemized bills for LTC services
- Plan of care from licensed practitioner
- Receipts for expenses

CLAIM PAYMENT:
- Standard processing: 30-45 days
- Expedited processing available for terminal illness
- Payment options: Lump sum, installments, life income, interest option
- Cash value paid with death benefit if no loans outstanding

NON-FORFEITURE OPTIONS:
If policy lapses:
1. Cash surrender value
2. Reduced paid-up insurance
3. Extended term insurance

POLICY EXCHANGE:
- Can exchange for different policy type within first 2 years
- No medical underwriting required
- Cash value transfers to new policy
