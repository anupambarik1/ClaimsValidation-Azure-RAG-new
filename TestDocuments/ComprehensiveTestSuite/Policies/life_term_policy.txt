SECURE LIFE TERM INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: TERM-2024-56789
Policyholder: Jennifer Williams
Insured: Jennifer Williams
Effective Date: February 15, 2024
Policy Type: 20-Year Level Term Life Insurance

COVERAGE AMOUNT: $500,000
Monthly Premium: $85.00
Policy Term: 20 years (expires February 15, 2044)
Beneficiaries: 
  - Primary: David Williams (spouse) - 100%
  - Contingent: Emma Williams (daughter), Lucas Williams (son) - 50% each

DEATH BENEFIT:
Standard Death Benefit: $500,000
- Payable upon death of insured from any cause after contestability period
- No cash value accumulation
- Level premium for entire 20-year term
- Guaranteed death benefit amount

COVERED CAUSES OF DEATH:
- Natural causes
- Accidental death
- Illness or disease
- Terminal illness (accelerated benefit available)

ACCELERATED DEATH BENEFIT RIDER:
- Available if diagnosed with terminal illness (life expectancy < 12 months)
- Can access up to 75% of death benefit while living
- Remaining 25% paid to beneficiaries upon death
- No additional premium for this rider

EXCLUSIONS (First 2 Years - Contestability Period):
- Suicide within first 2 years: Premium refund only
- Material misrepresentation on application: Policy may be voided
- After 2 years: Incontestable except for premium non-payment

PREMIUM PAYMENT:
- Due monthly on 15th of each month
- Grace period: 31 days
- Policy lapses if premium not paid within grace period
- Reinstatement possible within 5 years with evidence of insurability

CONVERSION PRIVILEGE:
- Can convert to permanent life insurance without medical exam
- Available anytime before age 65 or end of term
- Must convert to equivalent or lesser face amount
- Premium based on age at conversion

CLAIM SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS:
- Certified death certificate (original)
- Completed claim form signed by beneficiary
- Proof of beneficiary identity (government-issued ID)
- Policy document (original if available)
- Physician's statement of cause of death
- If accidental death: Police report, autopsy report if performed
- If death outside US: Consular Report of Death

CLAIM PAYMENT:
- Standard processing: 30 days from receipt of complete documentation
- Payment options: Lump sum, installments, interest-bearing account
- Interest accrues from date of death to payment date

CONTESTABLE CLAIMS (Within 2 Years):
Additional requirements:
- Complete medical records from all treating physicians
- Attending physician statement
- Medical examiner's report
- Prescription drug history
- Any relevant hospital records

EXCLUSIONS & LIMITATIONS:
- War or act of war (undeclared or declared)
- Aviation (except as fare-paying passenger on scheduled airline)
- Participation in riot or insurrection
- Commission of felony

FRAUD WARNING:
Any person who knowingly presents false or fraudulent claim for payment of loss is guilty of a crime and may be subject to fines and confinement in prison.
