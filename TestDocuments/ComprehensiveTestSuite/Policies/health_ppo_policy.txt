LIBERTY CHOICE PPO INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: PPO-2024-45621
Policyholder: Sarah Martinez
Effective Date: March 1, 2024
Policy Type: Preferred Provider Organization (PPO)

COVERAGE DETAILS:
Annual Deductible: 
  - In-Network: $1,000 per individual / $2,000 per family
  - Out-of-Network: $2,500 per individual / $5,000 per family
Out-of-Pocket Maximum:
  - In-Network: $5,000 per individual / $10,000 per family
  - Out-of-Network: $10,000 per individual / $20,000 per family

PRIMARY CARE SERVICES (In-Network):
- Office visits: $20 copay
- Preventive care: 100% covered
- Annual wellness exam: 100% covered
- Immunizations: 100% covered
- Lab work: 90% covered after deductible

PRIMARY CARE SERVICES (Out-of-Network):
- Office visits: 60% covered after deductible
- Lab work: 60% covered after deductible

SPECIALIST SERVICES:
- In-Network: $40 copay (no referral needed)
- Out-of-Network: 60% covered after deductible
- Diagnostic imaging (MRI, CT): 80% in-network, 60% out-of-network after deductible

HOSPITALIZATION:
- Inpatient (In-Network): 85% covered after deductible
- Inpatient (Out-of-Network): 60% covered after deductible
- Emergency room: $500 copay (waived if admitted), covered at in-network rate
- Ambulance: 100% covered for emergency
- Surgical procedures: 85% in-network after deductible

SURGICAL BENEFIT SCHEDULE (In-Network):
- Outpatient minor surgery: 85% after deductible
- Inpatient moderate surgery: 85% after deductible
- Major surgical procedures: 85% after deductible
- Reconstructive surgery (medically necessary): 85% after deductible

PRESCRIPTION DRUGS (In-Network):
- Generic: $15 copay
- Preferred brand: $50 copay
- Non-preferred brand: $90 copay
- Specialty medications: 25% coinsurance (max $250)

MATERNITY CARE:
- Prenatal care: $20 copay per visit (in-network)
- Delivery: 85% covered after deductible
- Postpartum care: $20 copay per visit

MENTAL HEALTH & SUBSTANCE ABUSE:
- Outpatient therapy: $30 copay (in-network)
- Inpatient treatment: 80% covered after deductible
- Substance abuse rehabilitation: 80% covered (30-day limit per year)

DURABLE MEDICAL EQUIPMENT:
- Wheelchairs, walkers, crutches: 80% covered after deductible
- Home oxygen equipment: 80% covered after deductible
- CPAP machines: 80% covered after deductible

EXCLUSIONS:
- Cosmetic surgery (except post-mastectomy reconstruction)
- Weight loss programs
- Infertility treatments
- Experimental or investigational procedures
- Services not medically necessary

PRE-AUTHORIZATION REQUIREMENTS:
- All inpatient admissions (except emergency)
- Outpatient surgeries over $5,000
- MRI, CT, PET scans
- Durable medical equipment over $500
- Home health care
- Failure to obtain pre-authorization may result in 50% reduction in benefits

CLAIMS SUBMISSION:
- Submit within 120 days of service
- Include itemized bills, explanation of medical necessity
- Out-of-network claims require submission by member
- Appeals accepted within 180 days of denial
