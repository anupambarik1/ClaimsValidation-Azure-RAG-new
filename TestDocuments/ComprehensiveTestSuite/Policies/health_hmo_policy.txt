HEALTHGUARD HMO INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: HMO-2024-78945
Policyholder: Michael Thompson
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policy Type: Health Maintenance Organization (HMO)

COVERAGE DETAILS:
Annual Deductible: $1,500 per individual / $3,000 per family
Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $6,000 per individual / $12,000 per family
Primary Care Copay: $25
Specialist Copay: $50
Emergency Room: $250 copay (waived if admitted)

PRIMARY CARE SERVICES:
- Office visits: $25 copay, unlimited visits
- Preventive care: 100% covered, no copay
- Annual physical exam: 100% covered
- Immunizations: 100% covered
- Lab work (in-network): 100% covered after deductible

SPECIALIST SERVICES:
- Specialist consultation: $50 copay (referral required)
- Diagnostic tests: 80% covered after deductible
- X-rays and imaging: 80% covered after deductible
- Physical therapy: $35 copay, maximum 30 visits per year

HOSPITALIZATION:
- Inpatient hospital stay: 90% covered after deductible
- Emergency room: $250 copay, waived if admitted
- Ambulance: 100% covered for emergency
- Surgery (inpatient): 90% covered after deductible

SURGICAL PROCEDURES BENEFIT SCHEDULE:
- Minor surgery ($1,000 - $3,000): 90% after deductible
- Intermediate surgery ($3,001 - $8,000): 90% after deductible
- Major surgery ($8,001 - $25,000): 90% after deductible
- Complex surgery (over $25,000): 85% after deductible

PRESCRIPTION DRUGS:
- Generic: $10 copay
- Preferred brand: $40 copay
- Non-preferred brand: $75 copay
- Specialty drugs: 20% coinsurance, max $200 per prescription

MATERNITY CARE:
- Prenatal visits: $25 copay per visit
- Delivery and postpartum: 90% covered after deductible
- C-section: 90% covered after deductible

MENTAL HEALTH:
- Outpatient therapy: $35 copay per session
- Inpatient psychiatric care: 80% covered after deductible

EXCLUSIONS:
- Cosmetic procedures (unless medically necessary)
- Experimental treatments
- Services outside network (except emergency)
- Over-the-counter medications
- Alternative medicine (acupuncture, chiropractor beyond 12 visits)

NETWORK REQUIREMENTS:
- Must use in-network providers for coverage
- Referrals required for specialist visits
- Pre-authorization required for: MRI, CT scans, surgeries, hospital admissions
- Out-of-network services not covered except emergencies

CLAIMS SUBMISSION:
- File claims within 90 days of service
- Include itemized bill, diagnosis codes, provider information
- Pre-authorization denial results in claim denial
- Appeal process available within 180 days
