LIFE INSURANCE POLICY - STANDARD TERM LIFE

Policy Coverage Terms:
- Coverage Amount: Up to $1,000,000
- Premium: Based on age, health, and coverage amount
- Policy Term: 10, 20, or 30 years

Covered Events:
1. Death due to natural causes
2. Death due to accidental causes
3. Critical illness rider (optional): Cancer, Heart Attack, Stroke
4. Terminal illness acceleration: Up to 50% of death benefit if diagnosed with terminal illness

Exclusions:
1. Suicide within first 2 years of policy
2. Death during commission of a crime
3. Death due to war or acts of terrorism
4. Fraudulent information on application

Critical Illness Coverage:
- Cancer diagnosis: Pays 100% of critical illness benefit ($50,000 minimum)
- Heart attack: Requires hospitalization of at least 3 days
- Stroke: Must result in permanent neurological deficit
- Maximum benefit: $100,000 per critical illness event

Claim Requirements:
- Death certificate
- Completed claim form
- Medical records (if applicable)
- Police report (for accidental death)
- Processing time: 30-45 days from receipt of complete documentation

Premium Payment:
- Monthly, quarterly, or annual payment options
- Grace period: 31 days
- Policy lapses if premium not paid within grace period
